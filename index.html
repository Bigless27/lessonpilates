ABOUT ME

I have a passion for Pilates!

I love motivating others to exercise and I have been doing this for the last decade. A STOTT certified instructor in matt and reformer 1 and 2, my education has extended to all equipment in the Pilates studio including Cadillac, chair, and barrel

Being an MSU graduate and mom of 3 has allowed me to pursue other chanlleges, but I always return to my first love: the Pilates method of exercise and helping others reach their fitness goals.

My classes are challenging yet fun and I have always emphasized anatomy and injury prevention. I go beyond the physical movement and help one connect with body knowledge and brain power along with a fair amount of sweat added!

Put on your workout clothes and come on in to relieve stress and forget about your daily troubles! You'll be glad you did!

Susan Lesson (That's Me!)
